Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Data Accessed

Board Export reads card data from the Trello board you have installed it on. This includes:

  • Card name, card number, description, URL, position, due date
  • List name
  • Labels and members
  • Checklists and check items
  • Comments and activity history
  • Attachments metadata (name, URL, date)

Data Storage and Processing

All processing happens entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server — it is assembled locally and offered to you as a downloadable file. Nothing is stored, logged, or transmitted by this Power-Up.

Third-Party Sharing

No data is shared with any third party. You control what you do with the exported file.

Data Retention

No data is retained server-side. The exported file exists only on your device and is under your control.

Contact

This is an open-source Power-Up. Issues may be filed on the GitHub repository.
